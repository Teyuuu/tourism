Event Registration Requirements

Review the essential requirements needed for your event application. Click on each item for detailed information.

Copy of Letter

▼

Submit a copy of the letter received by the Barangay that has jurisdiction over the venue where your event will be held. This letter serves as acknowledgment from the local barangay government.

Program/Schedule of Activities

▼

Provide a detailed program or schedule of activities for your event, including timeline, activities, speakers, performers, and any other relevant event details.

Traffic Management Plan

▼

Submit a comprehensive traffic management plan from BTMD (Bacoor Traffic Management Department) detailing road closures, alternative routes, parking arrangements, traffic flow control measures, and coordination with local traffic authorities.

Emergency Management Plan

▼

Present a detailed emergency management plan from BDRRMO (Bacoor Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office) including evacuation procedures, emergency exits, first aid stations, fire safety measures, and contact information for emergency services.
